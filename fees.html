Fees and Resources

I am happy to provide fee quotes to people in need of legal or notarial services. Give me a call: you will be able to speak directly to me and get a quick response to your enquiry. I offer outstanding rates for my services.

Out of Town Clients


I am able to offer my services to people who live outside the Victoria area. The miracles of modern technology have made the world a much smaller place. I have set up my practice, and equipped myself, to deal with my clients and their transactions regardless of where they may be located. In particular, I am able to handle B.C. real estate transactions (purchase, sale or refinance) for people regardless of where they may live when the transaction occurs.
