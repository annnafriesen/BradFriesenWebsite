About Brad

I am a lawyer and notary public based in Oak Bay, British Columbia. I received a Bachelor of Arts (1983) and a Law Degree from U.B.C. (1986). My practice is focused on the issues that people face on a regular basis: whether you are buying or selling your home, preparing a will, looking to incorporate your new business, creating an agreement with your soon-to-be spouse or handling an estate – these are all matters for which I am able to offer my assistance.

Commitment to my clients


The hallmark of my community-based service is to provide high quality, reliable legal services to my clients. My commitment to you is to be readily accessible, highly efficient, and reasonably priced. I measure my success by the degree of satisfaction achieved by my clients, and by the contribution I have made to the community in which I live and work.

Based in BC


I love BC! I’ve spent my life in this province. I have lived and worked in Vancouver, the Fraser Valley and the Okanagan. I’m glad to call Victoria and this Province my home. I am married and a father of three fabulous children.

Please feel free to contact me, and I’d be happy to be of service to you.
